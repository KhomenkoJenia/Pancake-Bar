History
Nascence
The pancake has a very long history and featured in cookery books as far back as 1439. The tradition of tossing or flipping them is almost as old: “And every man and maide doe take their turne, And tosse their Pancakes up for feare they burne.”
Popularity
In the USA pancakes became very popular in the middle of the 20th century. They began to be prepared not only at home, but also in restaurants because it is ideal dish for breakfast, lunch or dinner. We follow all the traditions and cook pancakes according to the traditional American recipe, which makes them so tasty and unforgettable !

Celebrating
In 1445 a woman of Olney heard the shriving bell while she was making pancakes and ran to the church in her apron, still clutching her frying pan. The Olney pancake race is now world famous. The object of the race is to get to the finishing line first, carrying a frying pan with a cooked pancake in it and flipping the pancake as you run.
